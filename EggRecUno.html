Rolled Eggs, A Great Breakfast

Amount Ingredient
2 Eggs
1 pinch Salt
1 pinch Pepper
2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
A few squirts Ketchup or Siracha
  1. In a small bowl scramble together the eggs salt and pepper.
  2. In a small sauce pan pour the vegetable oil and heat on medium.
  3. Pour a thin layer of egg such that it covers the entire pan.
  4. Let the egg cook on the bottom then take a spatula and begin folding the egg on itself, such that it is like a swiss roll.
  5. Push the rolled egg to one side of the pan and poor another layer of egg onto the pan. The new egg should touch the rolled part.
  6. Repeat the rolling process and add new egg until it is all used up.
  7. Plate and slice into mini rolls.
  8. Usually you add ketchup on top, but I prefer siracha.

Eggs in a Basket, A Amazing Snack

Amount Ingredient
1 Egg
1 pinch Salt
1 pinch Pepper
2 tbsp Butter
  1. Butter both sides of the bread.
  2. Use a cup (or fun cookie cutter!) to cut a hole in the middle of the bread. Save the center.
  3. In a pan melt about 1 tbsp of butter on medium heat.
  4. Place the holey toast in the pan.
  5. Crack an egg into the whole of the toast and add salt and pepper.
  6. Wait until the toast is golden brown on the bottom and then flip over.
  7. At this point add the hole from the bread to the pan as well and cook both sides to a golden brown.
  8. When everything is golden brown remove and serve.
